Description of Descriptive_Statistics_Quiz_Histogram.png

This histogram shows the frequency of car ownership by faculty members. Sixty-four faculty members were surveyed and asked how many cars each owned. The number of cars owned is displayed along the X-axis, with the relative frequency of each response displayed on the Y-axis. The results are as follows:

  • Zero percent of the faculty members reported owning no car at all
  • Approximately 15% of the faculty members reporting owning exactly one car
  • Approximately 45% of the faculty members reporting owning exactly two cars
  • Approximately 25% of the faculty members reporting owning exactly three cars
  • Zero percent of the faculty members reported owning between four and six cars
  • Approximately 15% of the faculty members reporting owning exactly seven cars
